Mr Price has launched its Local Business Love initiative to connect customers with small and local businesses. Brand project manager Zanthea van Zyl says the company felt the need to support local businesses during these uncertain times.
Mr Price Group Limited, is a publicly traded retail company based in South Africa. Founded in 1985, Mr Price opened in 1987 after the owners bought a controlling interest in John Orrs Holdings, whose trading divisions at the time were The Hub and Miladys.
Mr Price is a fashion-leading clothing, footwear, cosmetics and accessories retailer that offers on-trend and differentiated merchandise at extraordinary value to ladies, men and kids
