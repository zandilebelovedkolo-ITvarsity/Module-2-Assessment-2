Mugg & Bean is a full-service, & On-The-Move restaurant, coffee-themed franchise restaurant chain originating from South Africa.
The restaurant chain was founded in 1996 by Ben Filmalter after a visit to a Chicago coffee shop in the early 1990s inspired him to open a similar restaurant in South Africa.
Rewards BeansYou'll earn Beans with any and all purchases at any and all Mugg & Bean restaurants or Mugg & Bean On-The-Move. To collect your Beans, just use the app to scan the QR code on your bill. Your Beans will be added to your balance, so that you can use them to pay for your favourites and settle your bill.
